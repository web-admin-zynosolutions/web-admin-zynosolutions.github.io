Z-800 Series

The Z-800 Infusion Pump Series is Zyno Medical’s flagship large volume infusion pump platform. These pole-mounted infusion pumps are “purpose-driven”, designed to be a full-featured, full-performance range, state-of-the-art IV infusion pumps. The purpose-driven design objectives were met to create a diverse range of clinical use-cases necessary for the infusion pump environment. This provides a pump that is intelligent with multi-therapy programming modes that make it stand out clinically.
