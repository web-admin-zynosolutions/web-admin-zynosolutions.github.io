Z-800 Series

The Z-800 Infusion Pump Series is Zyno Medical’s flagship large volume infusion pump platform. These pole-mounted infusion pumps are “purpose-driven”, designed to be a full-featured, full-performance range, state-of-the-art IV infusion pumps. The purpose-driven design objectives were met to create a diverse range of clinical use-cases necessary for the infusion pump environment. This provides a pump that is intelligent with multi-therapy programming modes that make it stand out clinically.

Z-800F Z-800WF

Purpose Driven Design Features

performance elements of the Z-800 Infusion Pump Series which uniquely stand out for safety and ease-of use include:

Continuous Rate / Time / Volume Infusion

Custom Menu Configuration

Piggy-back Infusion

Blood Infusion

Intermittent Infusion

TPN Ramping Infusion

10-Step Programming Infusion

Custom Protocol Infusions 

Devices designed to make jobs easier and save administrators time. 

Collectively all of these “purpose-driven design” attributes create “Intelligent Infusion Solutions” in ways that cannot be found with other IV pumps. The current Z-800 Series manufactured by Zyno Medical includes the Z-800F and Z-800WF infusion pumps that are simple, safe, reliable and durable.
