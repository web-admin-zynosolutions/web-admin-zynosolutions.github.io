Nimbus™ II EMS

The Nimbus™ Ambulatory Infusion System combines the accuracy of an electronic infusion pump with a simple interface for quick intuitive programming. Nimbus™ is engineered to be forward-thinking to meet the evolving needs of the EMS world. Nimbus’s ultra compact and lightweight design leaves a small footprint in your vehicle all while retaining reliability and accuracy where it is needed most.
